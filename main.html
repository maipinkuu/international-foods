About the Festival

Discover a world of flavors at the 2024 International Food Festival. Featuring over 50 vendors from around the globe, live cooking demonstrations, and cultural performances, this festival is a must-attend for food lovers!

Festival Schedule

  • October 17: 10 AM - Opening Ceremony
  • October 18: 1 PM - Italian Cooking Demo
  • October 19: 12 PM - Japanese Cuisine Tasting
  • October 20: 3 PM - French Pastry Workshop
